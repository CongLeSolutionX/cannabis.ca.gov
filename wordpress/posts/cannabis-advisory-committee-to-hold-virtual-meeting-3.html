SACRAMENTO – The Bureau of Cannabis Control (Bureau) announced today that the Cannabis Advisory Committee will hold a virtual meeting on Wednesday, February 17, 2021.

The agenda for the meeting can be found on to the Bureau’s website at the following link: https://bcc.ca.gov/about_us/meetings/agendas/20210217_cac.pdf

To participate in the WebEx Events meeting on February 17, 2021, please log on to this website the day of the meeting: https://dca-meetings.webex.com/dca-meetings/onstage/g.php?MTID=ee88a92e52e0f592ff7ce5bbf5dd26a6f

The Cannabis Advisory Committee advises the Bureau, the California Department of Food and Agriculture, and the California Department of Public Health on the development of regulations that help protect public health and safety and do not perpetuate the illegal market for cannabis.
