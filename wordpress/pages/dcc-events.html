Upcoming events

Request a speaker for your event

You can request Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) staff to participate in your upcoming event by emailing info@cannabis.ca.gov.

We participate in events throughout the year, including as:

  • Panelist
  • Presenters
  • Guest speakers

We prioritize events that:

  • Help licensees understand rules and processes
  • Support equity applicants and licensees
  • Engage government and law enforcement partners
  • Support researchers studying cannabis

DCC does not provide financial support for events.
