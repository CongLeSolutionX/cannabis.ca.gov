What cannabis business is legal and where

Cannabis use is legal in California. But cities and counties can prohibit cannabis businesses in their areas.

What cannabis business is

Cannabis business activity includes:
  • Retail
  • Distribution
  • Testing
  • Manufacturing
  • Cultivation

Where business activities are allowed

The state has allowed cannabis business activities. But despite this, counties and cities can decide to:
  • Prohibit activities
  • Allow some activities and prohibit others
  • Allow activities, but set limits on them
And cities can decide to allow activities that their county prohibits. The result is a patchwork of areas where certain activities are legal and others are not.
