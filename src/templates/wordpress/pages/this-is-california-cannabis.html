The “This is California Cannabis” campaign features vibrant and compelling videos, photographs, and other materials that help tell the stories of our licensees and explain the benefits of the legal cannabis industry. These creative assets have been shared statewide through billboards, online digital ads, social media, and radio.

If you are interested in using these digital ads, 30-second public-service radio announcements, billboard art, social-media content, or videos in your own communications and media campaigns, please see the shareable content below or contact us by sending an email to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov.

Campaign toolkits

Join us in celebrating the dedicated cannabis business owners moving the state’s legal cannabis industry forward. Each of the toolkits listed below — one geared towards cannabis licensees and one for stakeholders — offers in-depth information about the campaign, shareable content and assets, and useful resources to help you take part in “This is California Cannabis.”

Licensee profiles

At the core of our “This is California Cannabis” campaign is the community of dedicated, innovative licensees who are paving the way for generations to come. Do you know outstanding licensed cannabis operators who deserve to be recognized? Are you a licensee interested in helping us reduce stigma and share your story? We’d like to feature these exceptional stories in our newsletter, social media posts, videos, and ad campaigns. Please send an email to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov with details about the license holder and a DCC communications team member will respond.

Video series: California Cannabis Conversations

We are proud to introduce “California Cannabis Conversations,” a new video series featuring hard-working and passionate licensed cannabis entrepreneurs throughout the state.

DCC Deputy Director of Equity and Inclusion, Eugene Hillsman, sits down with female entrepreneurs Amber Senter and Alicia Holcombe to discuss the necessity of equity programs and what’s needed to make the programs more effective.

Watch Cannabis Conversations episode 4 on YouTube

In this episode, we hear from cannabis cultivators Jim Moe and Andelain Roy on how the medicinal values of using cannabis to treat the painful symptoms associated with cancer and HIV/AIDS spurred them to make this their life’s passion and how they define community.

Watch Cannabis Conversations episode 2 on YouTube
