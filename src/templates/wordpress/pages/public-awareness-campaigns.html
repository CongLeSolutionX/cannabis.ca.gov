This is California Cannabis

2020-present

This is California Cannabis focuses on the cannabis market. The campaign:

  • Promotes the legal cannabis market
  • Highlights hardworking members of the legal cannabis community
  • Promotes the benefits of a strong and safe legal market

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Email newsletters
  • Videos

Get #weedwise

2012-present

Get #weedwise educates consumers about:

  • The risks associated with illicit products
  • How to identify a licensed retailer
  • It also warns businesses of the consequences of operating without a license.

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Presentations
